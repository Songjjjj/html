Exploring the Relationship Between Income and Fitness in NYC

A comprehensive study of how income levels influence gym distribution and fitness habits across New York City neighborhoods

View Data Analysis Learn More

Key Research Findings

Strong Correlation

87% positive correlation between neighborhood income levels and gym density

Price Variation

Monthly gym membership costs range from $20 to $500 across different districts

Distribution Pattern

Higher concentration of luxury gyms in areas with average income above $100,000

Community Impact

Community fitness centers play vital role in lower-income neighborhoods
